Checklist:
Launch your first Unboared game night
Everything you need to make your first interactive event a success
1 — EQUIPMENT
A laptop (Windows or Mac)
An HDMI cable
A TV or large screen
A stable WiFi connection (or 4G/5G)
Optional: a speaker for atmosphere
2 — SETUP  (15 MIN BEFORE)
Create your account on console.unboared.com
Connect the laptop to the TV via HDMI
Open console.unboared.com in fullscreen (F11)
Create a session and check the QR code
Test the QR code with your own phone
Adjust the volume if using the blind test
3 — YOUR GAMES FOR THE NIGHT
Blind Test — To break the ice
GeoLoc — For a curious and cultured audience
P'tit Bac — The classic that always works
UnQuizz — If you've prepared a custom quiz
Bomber Kitten — To end the night with action
4 — PROMOTE THE EVENT
Announce on social media (2 and 1 day before)
Display a flyer in the venue with the concept
Brief your staff: "Scan the QR code on the TV!"
Prepare a reward for the winner
(free drink, discount, free night out…)
5 — DURING THE EVENT
Launch the 1st game ~30 min after opening
Announce it out loud or on your live stories
Switch games every 20 to 30 minutes
Encourage guests to scan — it's simple!
Give the reward to the winner in front of everyone
6 — AFTER THE EVENT
Check your stats at b2b.unboared.com
Note which game generated the most engagement
Retrieve collected emails if the option is active
Plan the next event and communicate about it
Share your feedback at contact@unboared.com
Pro tip:

Always start with Blind Test or GeoLoc. Accessible to everyone,
they immediately create atmosphere — even with few people at the start of the evening.
